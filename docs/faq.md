
# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Account & Subscription

### How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the top right corner of the homepage. Follow the prompts to enter your email, create a password, and complete your profile.

### Is there a free trial available?
Yes, MedMaster offers a 7-day free trial for new users. You'll have access to select courses and features during this period.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription anytime by going to your Profile > Subscription Settings > Cancel Subscription. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

### Can I switch between subscription plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

## Courses & Learning

### How do I enroll in a course?
Browse the course catalog, select a course you're interested in, and click the "Enroll Now" button on the course detail page. Follow the prompts to complete enrollment.

### Can I download course materials for offline use?
Yes, most courses offer downloadable resources such as PDF notes, practice questions, and supplementary materials that you can save for offline reference.

### How is my progress tracked?
MedMaster automatically tracks your progress as you complete lectures and modules. Your progress is displayed visually throughout the platform and saved so you can resume where you left off.

### How do bookmarks work?
While watching a lecture, you can click the bookmark icon to mark important points in the video. Your bookmarks are saved and can be accessed from the lecture page to quickly jump to those sections.

### Will I receive a certificate upon completing a course?
Yes, upon completing all required modules and lectures in a course, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can download, print, or share digitally.

## Technical Questions

### Which browsers are supported?
MedMaster works best on recent versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for optimal performance.

### Can I use MedMaster on my mobile device?
Yes, MedMaster is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. You can access all features through your mobile browser.

### What should I do if a video won't play?
If you're experiencing playback issues:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Ensure your browser is updated
5. Try a different browser
If problems persist, please contact our support team.

### How are my data and privacy protected?
MedMaster takes data protection seriously. We use encryption for all sensitive information, never share your personal data with third parties without consent, and comply with relevant privacy regulations. For details, please review our Privacy Policy.

## Webinars

### How do I register for a webinar?
Visit the Webinars page, browse upcoming events, and click "Register" on the webinar you wish to attend. You'll receive a confirmation email with joining instructions.

### If I can't attend a live webinar, will I have access to the recording?
Yes, recordings of webinars are typically made available within 24-48 hours after the live event and can be accessed from the Webinars section.

### Can I ask questions during webinars?
Yes, live webinars include a Q&A feature that allows you to submit questions to the presenter.

## Support

### How can I get help if I have a problem?
For technical issues or questions about your account, please contact our support team via:
- Email: support@medmaster.com
- In-app chat support (available during business hours)
- Help Center (accessible from your dashboard)

### Is there a community forum?
Yes, MedMaster has a community forum where learners can discuss courses, share study tips, and connect with peers. You can access the forum from your dashboard.
