
# MedMaster Features

MedMaster offers a comprehensive suite of features designed specifically for medical education.

## Learning Content

### Courses
- Structured learning paths covering specific medical topics
- Multiple modules with video lessons, readings, and assessments
- Progress tracking and completion certificates
- Downloadable resources and supplementary materials

### Webinars
- Live educational sessions with medical experts
- Recorded webinars accessible on-demand
- Interactive Q&A and discussion opportunities
- Specialized topics and case studies

## Learning Tools

### Video Player
- Adaptive streaming quality based on connection speed
- Playback speed control (0.5x to 2x)
- Volume adjustment and mute options
- Progress tracking and automatic resume
- Bookmarking feature for important segments

### Progress Tracking
- Course completion percentage
- Individual lesson progress indicators
- Resume learning from where you left off
- Completed content indicators

## User Experience

### Responsive Design
- Seamless experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- Adaptive layouts for optimal viewing on any screen size

### Personalized Dashboard
- Overview of enrolled courses
- Progress statistics and achievements
- Recommended content based on user interests
- Upcoming webinars and events

## Community and Support

### Reviews and Ratings
- Course and instructor ratings
- Detailed user reviews and feedback
- Star-based rating system

### Certification
- Course completion certificates
- Verified credentials for professional development
- Shareable achievements
